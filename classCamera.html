Construct a camera with transformation, projection, and focal parameters.

Parameters
i_originThe position of the camera origin, or eye.
i_lookAtThe point of camera eye is focused onto.
i_viewUpThe up-vector of the camera.
i_verticalFovThe vertical field of view, in degrees. This is the angle formed from the camera origin towards the viewport height.
i_aspectRatioRatio of the width against the height of the rendered image.
i_aperatureThe diameter of the camera lens, or hole where light flows in.
i_focalDistanceThe distance between the camera origin and the focal plane where objects are in perfect focus.
